Tip Sheet

Tips and Tricks for Starting a Club

Using these pro-tips, spend some time planning how to develop your Webmaker club and facilitate learning in an informal setting. Then get going and #teachtheweb!

Made by Laura.

Collaborate

People = Fun

Find people to work with who you find interesting. They can help you plan, facilitate, promote, document & share your experience.

Coordinate

Dates, Space, Stuff, People

Pick your first time & date. Find a cafe, library, or other space where you can be comfortable. Figure out what materials you need (food, drinks, post its, computers, etc), & list people you want to invite.

Deliberate

Agenda and Feedback

Figure out what you want to teach. Gather input from potential learners, and keep in mind the themes you want to cover. Check out the Clubs curriculum for some input.

Advocate

Online and Locally

Share your club plans on social media, set up a web presence, define a hashtag, post flyers and tell any and everyone about your club both online and in your local community.
