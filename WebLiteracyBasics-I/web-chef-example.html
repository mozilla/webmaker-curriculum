How to Connect with Webmakers

by Laura

Following this recipe will help you expand your online network and learn about the Mozilla Community.

Discourse, Twitter, Webmaker Newsgroup, Google + and the Webmaker blog

  1. Introduce yourself to the community discussion forum
  2. Tweet a picture of something you made using the hashtag #teachtheweb on Twitter.
  3. Join the Webmaker newsgroup and introduce yourself via email.
  4. Connect on Google+ and share your thoughts on Connected Learning
  5. Visit the Webmaker blog and comment on a post you found interesting.
