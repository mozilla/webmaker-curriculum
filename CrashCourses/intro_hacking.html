Warm Up

Define Hacking

What this page is

Find out what your learners preconceived notions are by asking them to define "hacking" before you've introduced the concept.

To the XRay Goggles Kit To the TASCasaurus Kit View other kits

Steps

  1. Ask participants to raise their hands if they use the Internet, ask what their favorite web page is and chart responses.

  2. In teams of 2-4, ask participants to take turns bringing up their favorite web page on their computer. Have participants tell the group what they like about the page.

  3. Ask participants if there is something they would change about the page and chart responses.

  4. Ask participants if they have ever heard of the term hacking, what they think it means. Then explain that they are going to experience hacking through dance.

Materials

  • Poster Paper
  • Markers
