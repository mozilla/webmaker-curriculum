What is this kit all about?

Through the activities in this Kit, learners will gain an understanding on how media has influence over culture and society, and cultures and societies have influence over shaping media. They'll use Popcorn Maker to remix context and bring stories to life.

Learning objectives

Learners will understand:

  • Media has influence over culture and society, and cultures and societies have influence over shaping media.

Learners will be able to :

  • Use Popcorn Maker to pull dynamic data from the web into a video.
  • Recontextualize a story by adding data and unique voice to it.
  • Discuss media's implications on society and culture
  • Create storyboards and schematics.
  • Use keyboard shortcuts (i.e. for copying and pasting).
  • Right-click and scroll with the mouse.
  • Search for and find content to remix.

What you'll make together

Learners will use Popcorn Maker to make their own web native films and explore larger concepts on the topic. They will collaborate to make critical commentary and use assets from the web to tell a story of their own.

Preparation

Make sure computers have the latest version of Firefox or Chrome on them. Help learners set up a Persona account before the session, so that they can save their work.

Discussion Questions

  • How does media influence society?
  • How is the web different from the other mass mediums?
  • How did feedback help you iterate and improve your project?
  • Why is it important to have open access to the Internet?
  • Why is it important that the web is designed by many individuals?
  • Why is it important for websites to invite participation?

Assessment criteria

Did the learner show their ability to:
  • understand and use recontextualization
  • understand and use Popcorn
  • find content to remix and reuse
  • discuss media's implications on society and culture
  • change the story of a piece of media by adding information to it
  • use a search engine to find specific content
  • find the content he/she was looking for
  • use creativity
  • work with others
