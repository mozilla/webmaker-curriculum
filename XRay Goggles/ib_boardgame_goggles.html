Activity

Hack a Boardgame

Participants change the rules of a popular boardgame to hack the game play.

Made by Laura Hilliger

Steps for the Activity

  1. Everyone breaks into groups of no more than five people and finds a space to work in.

  2. Facilitators explain that we will be changing the rules of the game to form a new game.

  3. Groups work to redefine the rules of the boardgame either by playing the game and coming up with new rules as they go, or by creating a new set of rules before starting to play.

  4. Collect the game rules that participants come up and post them on the wall.

Materials

  • A few board games. Monopoly works really well, but any boardgame can be hacked.
  • Other fun, random components like playing cards, toys, figurines, legos, clay, paint, etc.
